ABA HS Coding Club

A student society excited to foster a passion for programming, problem-solving and hacking!

Scroll Down!

By the students, for the students.

Learning to code is like gaining a superpower — turning you from a consumer of technology into a creator. It shouldn’t be taught like a class — it should be a creative, inclusive space. To foster this environment, our club is student-led & members make self-directed projects.

Meeting Times

Every Sunday --- second break --- @ Mr. Nik's room!

What to get?

Just your laptop, some food and your awesome ideas!

What We Actually Do

In Our Sessions

Free Python Workshop (+ other workshops!)

By joining our club, you are getting access to a step-by-step simple weekly lessons for getting introduced to python! We also aim to introduce HTML, CSS & JS for web dev!

Real World Opportunities

We connect you with real world professional individuls which would help in gaining tech opportunities!

Competitions

We hold in-school events like quiz bowl, give you coding challenges and train for the CEMC competition!

Student Community

Coding is often seen as an isolating activity. The stereotype of a programmer is a person who sits alone in a dark room. It doesn't have to be this way—in the Coding Club we help you develop a collaborative style to coding.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up?

    Sign-up by clicking the "Login/Sign-up" button on the top right corner!

  • Can I join without any prior programming experience?

    Yes of course! This club holds session for students with no prior coding experience.

  • Who can attend sessions?

    ABA HSCC is open to all high school students!

  • How do I sign up for the Canadian/Beavers Computing Competition?

    You can just inform on of our leaders (Vaishnavi, Noorah or Janan) or speak to Mr. Poirier and we will sign you up!

  • Where can I access the session materials?

    Everything from our sessions are posted on our google classroom, so be sure to have joined it!
